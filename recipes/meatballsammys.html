Pepes Home Made Meatball Sammys

Pepes secret meatball sammy recipe

Pepe doesn't normally share his secret recipes with the public so consider yourself lucky. These meatball sammys

are to die for and really easy to make.They don't that all that long to make(bout an hour start to finish) and

will make you a hit among your frens.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. of hamburger
  • Little less than a 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • A tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 an onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 large egg
  • A tbsp of Parmesan cheese
  • A teaspoon of salt and pepper
  • A teaspoon of dried Italian herb seasoning
  • Large jar of your favorite Marinara sauce
  • 16 oz package of mozzarella cheese
  • Package of your favorite sub rolls

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Grease a baking sheet with either cooking spray or olive oil.
  3. Mix the hamburger, breadcrumbs, onion, garlic, egg, Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper, and herbs together in a mixing bowl.
  4. Shape the meatball mix into golfball size balls, placing them onto the baking sheet as you form them about an inch apart.
  5. Put them into the oven for about 15-20 minutes.
  6. After they're cool enough to handle, put some on a sub roll, cover with sauce, then the mozzzarella and put in microwave long enough to melt cheese.

That's it! Easy peasy! Enjoy!
