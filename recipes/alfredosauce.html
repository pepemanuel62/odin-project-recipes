Alfredo Sauce

This is the absolute BEST Alfredo Sauce!

I found this Alfredo sauce recipe on Allrecipes a few years ago and have made it too many times to count because it's that good and oh so easy to make.

It only takes about twenty minutes to make from start to finish and uses only five ingredients.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 ½ cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps:

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over low heat. Stir in cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add cheese and garlic; whisk continuously until heated through. Stir in parsley.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
