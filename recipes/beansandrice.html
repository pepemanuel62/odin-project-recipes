Pepes Home Made Beans and Rice

This ia a home made beans and rice recipe that ol' Pepe has been making for a few years.

This recipe is pretty tasty and of course theres a thousand variations but Pepe likes his with sausage and diced tomatoes. It only takes

20 minutes to cook the rice and oncee that's done just another 10 minutes to throw it all together.

Ingredients:

  • One cup of white rice
  • 1/2 teaspoon of chopped garlic
  • 1/4 cup of chopped onion
  • One 28 oz can of Rotel Original diced tomatoes and green chiles
  • One 15.5 oz can of Goya black beans(Pepe has an affinty for Goya kek)
  • One package of beef sausage
